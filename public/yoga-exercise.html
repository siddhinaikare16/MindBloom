Yoga for Anxiety and Stress

This gentle 27-minute practice is designed to help you find peace and calm. It focuses on mindful movement and deep breathing to soothe the nervous system and release tension from the body. Perfect for when you're feeling overwhelmed.

  • Focus: Calming, gentle movement, and breathwork.
  • Key Poses: Child's Pose, Gentle Twists, Seated Forward Bends.
  • Ends with: A soothing Savasana (final relaxation).

15-Minute Mood-Boosting Cardio

Get your heart rate up and release some endorphins with this fun and energetic 15-minute cardio session. No equipment is needed, just a positive attitude to boost your mood for the day!

  • Warm-Up (3 mins): Light jogging in place, arm circles, leg swings.
  • Workout (10 mins): Circuits of Jumping Jacks, High Knees, Butt Kicks, and Squats.
  • Cool-Down (2 mins): Gentle stretching for major muscle groups.

5-Minute Morning Yoga Flow

Step 1: Cat-Cow Stretch - Start on all fours. Inhale as you drop your belly and look up (Cow). Exhale as you round your spine and tuck your chin (Cat). Repeat 5 times.

Step 2: Downward-Facing Dog - From all fours, tuck your toes and lift your hips up and back. Pedal your feet to stretch your hamstrings. Hold for 30 seconds.

Step 3: Walk to Forward Fold - Slowly walk your feet to your hands. Let your head hang heavy. Hold for 30 seconds.

Step 4: Mountain Pose - Roll up to standing, one vertebra at a time. Stand tall with your feet together and arms at your sides. Take 3 deep breaths.

Simple Stretches for Stress Relief

1. Neck Rolls: Gently drop your chin to your chest and slowly roll your right ear towards your right shoulder. Hold for 15-30 seconds, then repeat on the left side.

2. Shoulder Shrugs: Inhale deeply and lift your shoulders up to your ears. Hold for a few seconds, then exhale and release them down. Repeat 5 times.

3. Seated Spinal Twist: Sit on the floor with your legs extended. Bend your right knee and place your right foot outside your left thigh. Twist your torso to the right, placing your left elbow on your right knee. Hold for 30 seconds and switch sides.
